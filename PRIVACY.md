# Privacy Policy for SnapScroll

**Effective Date: March 2026**

SnapScroll is built with a strict privacy-first philosophy.

1. **No Data Collection:** SnapScroll does not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell your personal data.
2. **Local Storage Only:** All data generated by the extension (such as the URLs you visit and your vertical scroll Y-coordinates) is stored exclusively on your local device using the browser's native `chrome.storage.local` API.
3. **No Third-Party Tracking:** We do not use Google Analytics, tracking pixels, or any external servers. Your browsing history and checkpoints remain 100% in your control.

By using SnapScroll, you consent to this local-only data processing. If you have any questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
